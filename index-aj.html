We currently support Microsoft Exchange, Office 365 and Google Apps, with apps for Windows, Mac, iPhone and Android.

Email Transformed

When you email another RedKix user, you'll see when they're online and replying to a message - just like you would in iMessage or Google Hangouts. And if they're not on RedKix, messages just go through as standard emails.

Groups For Inclusive Collaboration

Organize your team conversations into group channels. Use your existing email distribution lists, or create a new group around a project, topic, whatever! And unlike other tools, anyone with an email address can participate.

Chat for Fast, Easy Conversations

Subject-based conversations are great, but not always necessary. Chat with people 1–1 or in a group for those quick conversations (they don’t even have to be a RedKix user).

Unlike any other messaging or collaboration solution out there, we don't discriminate. RedKix users get a fast, real-time messaging experience, while the non-RedKix users participate via email without even knowing you've moved onto the 21st century (but you should still be sure to invite them)!
