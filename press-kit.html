Press Kit

RedKix is an email solution that brings order to the chaos of communication in the workplace.

There is great power in email from the organizational value of a single inbox to its universally open structure. Email is not dead, but with today’s fast-paced workflow, it has become broken.

Instead of your workflow being dictated by your inbox, we let you focus on what’s most important by organizing messages into group channels, from your favorite people and per topic.

RedKix connects to your existing work email account (Microsoft Exchange, Office 365, Google Apps), so there’s no need to sign up, you just sign in.

With RedKix, there are no barriers to communication - users can send and receive emails to anyone regardless of the email service they use.

By modernizing email and providing apps for both desktop and mobile, we remove the need for dedicated messaging apps.

You can use RedKix by yourself, or with your team, as a powerful, zero friction, real time communication and collaboration solution.
