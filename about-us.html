About Us

Our vision is to create a unified communication solution for the workplace that eliminates friction from team collaboration. We aim to remove the chaos and borders that exist today so that everyone can work in an organized and inclusive way.

RedKix is an email solution that brings order to the chaos of communication in the workplace.

Instead of your workflow being dictated by your inbox, we let you focus on what’s most important by organizing messages into group channels, from your favorite people and per topic.

RedKix works on top of your work email account, so there’s no need to sign up for anything, you just need to sign in.
